    Meet the Alumni

    The PCSS Alumni Committee was formed in 2001 with the goal of raising money for the school by holding annual Alumni Nights. The PCSS Alumni Scholarship Programme was created that same year, and it has helped many PCSS graduates. The Alumni association has also helped with many other things in the school, listed below...

    visit official website

    Sponsorship Opportunities

    • Flat Screen Display With the help of hosted Alumni Nights and the Alumni Scholarship Programme, several financial contributions allowed us to have the large, flat-screen video display, in the hallway near the front office.
    • Honour Board The Alumni Committee brought an other honour board with additional bronze bars, encapsulated with graduates' names. In 2015, the Alumni Association made a donation of $6000 toward updating the Honour Board, located in the hallway just outside the auditorium. The Honour Board is now up-to-date through 2015.
    • New Bleachers New bleachers were introduced outside the back entrance, beside the soccer field, thanks to the Alumni Night donations.
    • Courtyard Design In 2018, Alumni has helped PCSS with a landscaping project at the front of the school, engineered by Fossil Landscapes. This project also strived to create the beautiful courtyard PCSS has today!
    • New P.A. System The new P.A. system was a grand upgrade than compared to the previous one; key features included centralized programmable tone selection, built-in one stereo audio line output, programmable digital inputs for triggering special events etc...
