    WHAT'S IT ABOUT?

    Port Credit Secondary School will be celebrating its 100th anniversary on Saturday May 2nd, 2020! The event will contain several items such as opening ceremonies, formal luncheon, and a special reunion.

    visit alumni's website

    Luncheon

    There will be a luncheon with teachers, students, and other guests.

    Opening Ceremonies

    The event will have several opening ceremonies with special guests! Following the opening, several items for display will follow...

    Reunion Evening & School Tour

    A special school tour will be provided for all teachers, students, and guests. There will also be a special reunion evening with plenty of time for meeting and greeting.

    Talent Show & Choir

    There will be a talent show with a host of musicians playing in the auditorium, as well as a bunch of other performances. There are also decade rooms filled with lots of memorabilia.
